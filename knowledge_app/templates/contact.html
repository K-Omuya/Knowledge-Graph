Frequently Asked Questions

What is a knowledge graph?
A knowledge graph is a type of database that stores information in a graph structure, using nodes to represent entities and edges to represent relationships between them. In our system, we use knowledge graphs to visualize connections between course content and ideological elements.
How can I contribute to the knowledge graph?
After creating an account, you can add new knowledge nodes, create relationships between existing nodes, and participate in discussions. Your contributions help enrich the educational content for everyone.
Is there a mobile app available?
Currently, we offer a mobile-optimized web application that works well on smartphones and tablets. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future development.
How can I use the system for my own courses?
Educators can create custom learning paths and knowledge structures for their courses. Contact us for information about institutional licenses and custom implementations.
How is the content moderated?
Our system uses a combination of automated tools and human reviewers to ensure content quality and accuracy. Editors and administrators review contributions to maintain educational standards.
